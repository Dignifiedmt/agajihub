Game da AgajiHub

AgajiHub manhaja ce ta shirye-shiryen gaggawa da tallafin masu jinya wacce aka ƙirƙira domin ƙarfafa al'umma da ilimin ceton rai. An gina ta ne bisa manufar samar da hanyar taimakon gaggawa cikin harsunan gida (Hausa da Ingilishi) domin kowa ya iya koyon dabarun agajin farko cikin sauƙi.

Manhajar tana ɗauke da darussa masu mahimmanci kamar CPR, sarrafa zubar jini, kula da hanyar numfashi, da sauran su. Ana kuma samar da bidiyoyin horo da kuma damar yin rahoton gaggawa kai tsaye.

Manufarmu: Ƙarfafa al'umma da ilimin ceton rai, shirye-shiryen gaggawa, da kayan aikin taimakon farko.

Hangen Nesa: Kasancewa babbar manhajar shirye-shiryen gaggawa da tallafin masu jinya a Afirka.
